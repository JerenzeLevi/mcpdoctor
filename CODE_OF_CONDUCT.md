# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the code and the problem at hand.

- Be welcoming to newcomers and first-time contributors.
- Disagreements about technical approach are fine; personal attacks are not.
- Harassment, discriminatory language, or trolling will not be tolerated in issues, PRs, or discussions.

Violations can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block contributors who violate this code.
